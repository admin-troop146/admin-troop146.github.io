Troop 146 Participates in Community Clean-Up

New Scouts Welcomed at Fall Recruitment Night

Posted: October 15, 2023

We're thrilled to welcome five new scouts to Troop 146 following our successful Fall Recruitment Night! Both boys and girls from the Hudsonville area joined us to learn about scouting adventures. We look forward to many exciting times ahead.
